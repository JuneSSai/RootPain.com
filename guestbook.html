How To Leave Your Message

-Send a message to my email ( RootPain@420blaze.it )
with the email subject named "Guestbook Message"

-Write the message you'd like to leave on the email, and attach a name
and optionally, a catchphrase/slogan to go at the end of your message

-Your message can be as long as you want it to be, be aware that
if too big, it might get too big to read without zooming the page out

-The Guestbook Art dimension is 300x300 Pixels
You don't need to resize the image yourself but it's highly recommended

-The Guestbook Message PFP dimension is 100x100 Pixels

-If possible, send the images through an imgur link or any image hosting website
since my email provider is kinda fucky when it comes to attached images

-Almost anything goes, as long as it's not straight up illegal
Guestbook Messages are rarely rejected (for the most part)

-You can change your Guestbook Message every three weeks

-If you're still confused, here's an formatting example

Hover your mouse over an image to display it's message!
