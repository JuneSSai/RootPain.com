RootPain's Commentary

This is it. This is the real deal.
Do I need to say more? This is the WAD that gave name to this
fucking website and the name I've been using online for
a while now, although this wad is simple, I can't state
enough how much I love it and the reason behind it's creation

The WAD is very short and simple, you're a parasite, and you're inside a womb
your objective is to destroy the ovaries, but you don't have anywhere to go
or any weapons to finish your job, so how do you do it then?
It's a "Secret" ;)

The WAD itself is really short, you can finish it in one minute if you know
what you're doing, but something about it instantly called my attention like
a siren's calling, and I instantly fell in love with it

Something about the aesthetic of it, as odd as it sounds, feels really comfy
The atmospheric sound, the unsettling, yet familiar setting, the creepy sounds
and the hovering blood cells really gives this wad a one of a kind vibe that
I'm genuinely fond of

But I think the main reason why I love this WAD so much is because of the
amount of controversy this stupid thing created

When it first came out, people on the ZDoom forums really hated it
Some people were scared and concerned, but most were really angry, simply
because the WAD was edgy, and years later, it turns out that RootPain.WAD
really was a quick mod Zygo made as a joke, simply to get a laugh
and a reaction out of others, and the fact people took this silly thing
with such a visceral reaction when it was never meant to be anything
bigger is hysterical to me, I love the idea behind this WAD, and I love
everything about it, even how it's unecessarily edgy

I do think RootPain holds a bigger potential, though, and the creator
thought the same a while ago, to the point he was considering remaking
the whole WAD before he left the idea behind and just implemented it
as an extra level in his new Total Conversion mod, Solace Dreams
and although Solace Dreams does look pretty fantastic, I really wish
he expanded upon the RootPain WAD a little bit more, and I specially
wish he could fix the WAD so it could work on GZDoom aswell

But either way, give RootPain a try, I'm sure you'll find it at
least interesting, and that's more than I can say about a lot
of things I've seen.

I Will Live

RootPain
