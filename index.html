New Message - A Small Update

Eyy, it's me, RootPain, once again! Did ya' miss me, bub?
I still can't believe myself that the site is finally back up
once again, and it still doesn't feel real, mostly because
I'm impressed at myself for making a website like this one
(Yeah, I'm stroking my own ego, but with a good reason!)
But now that's it's out... what now?

If you've been keeping an close eye, you can see that I'm definitely still
working on the website, if you go to the Changelog section right now
you can see that I've been making small, although significant updates
on pretty much a daily basis, and these updates have been mostly visual
updates to make things a little more cohesive and stylized, but although
I've been refining a lot of these sections, some are still left unfinished
and some of the code definitely need some rework, mostly because I still
feel like there's always more that can be done, not only to improve
navigation and the style of the website, but to make it far more optimized
than it currently is, but I'm still looking into it as we speak, and I'm
definitely cooking more stuff as we speak, like the return of the dailies
(which now will be called weeklies since they're now a thing I'll update
weekly instead of daily, since I felt like making it a daily thing made
me run out of steam and ideas very quickly) and the return and remake of
old sections like the Blog and Downloads Sections!
So there's definitely a lot of stuff in the oven for you all, and I can say
it with confidence that it will probably won't take long for it to come out!

But now I also want to talk about something that's slowly coming back to me
like an unwanted memory that keeps coming back to haunt me

My anxiety and my depression

That's right folks, despite how raw and how much of a badass I am
I'm still a very lonely, anxious and honestly sad guy, and those things
were also a major factor that played over the fact that RootPain.com
took so long to come out

Although I got a job that I'm honestly very happy with, having a job
also means that a lot of the time and energy that I have is now exclusively
dedicated to that job, and even when I have free time, I'm usually too
tired and stressed to get out of my bed to do anything, so I tend to sleep
a lot because of it, and the little spare time that I have mostly goes to
the development of the website, leaving little time for myself and other
hobbies that I like.

Don't get me wrong, I love working on the website, but I've also been feeling
that I've been forcing myself to work on it way more than I actually want
or need to, and that's mostly because I don't want to give the impression
that I'm not interested or that I'm abandoning the website right after
releasing it to the public, I'm still very interested on it, I'm still
very proud of all of my work, and I still have a lot of ideas and concepts
that I want to implement in here, but I'm also feeling the overburn and
overwork finally hitting me, and not helping the case, is the fact that
I've been feeling increasingly sad and anxious over my own personal life

I've been sitting in front of my computer at times for hours trying to
finish a lot of sections and ideas, but not feeling that push to actually
make me want to put that 200% I've been doing with this whole thing
and I work on the idea that "Everything I do needs to scream passion"
if I'm not passionate about it, or I feel like I could have done better
if I tried just a little harder, I won't work on it, and at times, I'll
even completely scrap things if I don't feel proud of them in the long run

And I don't want to put anything on this website that I'm not proud of
and thus far, I'm proud of everything related to this website, and I want
to keep it like that, even if that means taking my sweet time to make sure
everything is exactly how I want it to be

So please, be patient while stuff is coming out, I'm still working on it
but I'll probably take a short break before going out with full force
again like I've been doing recently

And that's it for now
I hope you all stick around and see the things I have planned for this
website and you all

Thanks for supporting me and my endeavors, and I hope you all come back
for more and more

Also, sign the goddamn guestbook
It's pretty cool, I promise

Anyways, that's about it
I'll see you when the time comes.

Increasingly Sadder
